Cheesecake




Ingredients:

    For the Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar

    • For the Cheesecake Filling:

  • 4 (8-ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup sour cream
  • 2/3 cup heavy cream



Directions:

    

  1. Preheat the Oven:
Preheat your oven to 325°F (163°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan with butter or non-stick cooking spray.
    2. 

  2. Make the Crust:
In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar. Press the mixture into the 
bottom of the prepared springform pan, creating an even crust.
    3. 

  3. Bake the Crust:
Bake the crust in the preheated oven for 10 minutes. Remove it from the oven and let it cool while preparing the filling.
    4. 

  4. Prepare the Cheesecake Filling:
    a. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth and creamy.
    b. Add sugar and vanilla extract, and continue to beat until well combined.
    c. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
    d. Mix in sour cream and heavy cream until the batter is smooth and well combined..
    5. 

  5. Pour the Filling:
Pour the cream cheese filling over the baked crust in the springform pan, spreading it evenly.
    6. 

  6. Bake the Cheesecake:
Bake the cheesecake in the preheated oven for about 55-65 minutes or until the center is set and the top is slightly golden.
    7. 

  7. Cooling:
Allow the cheesecake to cool in the oven with the door ajar for about an hour. Then, refrigerate it for at least 4 hours or overnight.
