Tiramisu




Ingredients:

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • cup mascarpone cheese, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled to room temperature
  • 1/4 cup coffee liqueur (e.g., Kahl√∫a) - optional
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 24-30 ladyfinger cookies (savoiardi)
  • Cocoa powder, for dusting



Directions:

    

  1. Brew Coffee:
Brew a strong cup of coffee and let it cool to room temperature. If using coffee liqueur, you can add it to the cooled coffee.
    2. 

  2. Prepare Mascarpone Mixture:
    a. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together egg yolks and sugar until the mixture becomes pale and slightly thick.
    b. Add mascarpone cheese to the egg yolk mixture and beat until well combined.
    3. 

  3. Whip the Cream:
In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
    4. 

  4. Combine Mascarpone and Whipped Cream:
Gently fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture until smooth and well combined.
    5. 

  5. Assemble the Tiramisu:
    a. Dip each ladyfinger into the brewed coffee, making sure not to soak them too long.
    b. Arrange a layer of dipped ladyfingers in the bottom of a serving dish or individual glasses.
    c. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
    6. 

  6. Repeat Layers:
Repeat the process with another layer of dipped ladyfingers and the remaining mascarpone mixture.
    7. 

  7. Chill:
Cover the Tiramisu with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.Chilling allows the flavors to meld and 
the dessert to set.Before serving, dust the top of the Tiramisu with cocoa powder.
